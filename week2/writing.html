> Critique of Panic.com

Panic.com is your simple website out there for an up and coming app development company. Like many websites now adays the website is very minimalist. The homepage is simply a portal to learn about the different apps that the company produces. They also have quick method to request help with specific programs or just ask them a quick question on Twitter. It's a very accesable website and gets users where they want to go quickly. It should also be noted that Panic has a blog linked to their homepage as well. Again, the design here is simple and gives a feeling reminiscent of looking at paper pinned to a noteboard. Overall the website is very well put together; its simple, but informative.
